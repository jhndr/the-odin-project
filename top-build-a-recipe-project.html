Creamy Chocolate Fudge

Description

Creamy chocolate fudge is a rich, smooth confection made from a blend of melted chocolate, sugar, butter, and condensed milk. It has a soft, velvety texture with a deep, intense chocolate flavor. Often enjoyed in bite-sized squares, this indulgent treat is perfect for satisfying sweet cravings.

Creamy chocolate fudge is a decadent dessert made with melted chocolate, butter, and condensed milk. It’s smooth, rich, and indulgent, with a melt-in-your-mouth texture. Perfectly sweet and satisfying, it's often served in bite-sized squares and can be customized with add-ins like nuts or sea salt for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips or chopped chocolate
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Chopped nuts (walnuts, pecans, etc.)

Steps

  1. Melt chocolate chips, condensed milk, and butter in a saucepan over low heat, stirring constantly until smooth.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.
  3. Pour the mixture into a lined square pan and smooth the top.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator for 2-3 hours until firm.
  5. Cut into squares and enjoy!
